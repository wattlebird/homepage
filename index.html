Who I am

This is Ronnie Wang, a software development engineer specialized in data science and machine learning. I graduated as a Master from South China University of Technology and Polytech de l’Université de Nantes. I'm currently a full-time employee of Microsoft and working in Suzhou, China.
My research field during my master degree is online handwriting recognition. However, I touch more than one machine learning field, such as language understanding, search ads CTR prediction, recommendation system, computer vision and so on.
